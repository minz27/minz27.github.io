Thundermarch 2018

The north eastern part of India is known for being huge followers of western culture, music and sensibilities.
A big part of western culture which north eastern people have inculcated is the affinity to rock music.
North east people are said to be the biggest fans of rock and metal music.
National Institute of Technology, Silchar being a pioneer engineering institute of the country
has also contributed a lot in the development of the rock music scene.

Its biggest gift to the people of north-east has been Thundermarch.
Thundermarch being the annual rock fest of NIT Silchar
has time and again proven to be a major hit among the metalheads.
At present, Thundermarch is one of the most attended rock concerts in the North East
with footfall increasing every year. Our motto is "STAY HIGH, STAY BRUTAL"
and time and again Thundermarch has lived upto its motto as
everytime the event gets staged, the growls get louder,
the moshpits get crazier and the headbanging gets wilder.

Battle of Bands

Registrations Open!

Battle of Bands is the annual band competition where semi-professional bands from all over the country battle it out on stage to grab huge cash prizes, as well as grab the attention of the headliners by opening for them. Along with that, a fair share of goodies and mementos are also up for grabs.

Battle of Bands

Rules and Regulations

  • 1. The bands are required to provide links of two demo audio songs and a video link of their live performance. An original composition demo is a must.
  • 2. The selected bands are required to confirm their participation through email or phone call before "date tba". Each member of the band is required to pay an amount of Rs 100 for their fooding and lodging.
  • 3. Bands are required to bring their own gear and instruments. Only a basic drum kit will be provided by the organizers. The drummers can bring their own pedals and cymbals if required.
  • 4. There is no language/genre bar in 'Battle of Bands'.
  • 5. Use of abusive language, signs, or gestures on stage is strictly prohibited.
  • 6. During both prelims and finals the bands are required to play at least one original composition. failing which shall lead to negative marking.
  • 7. Pre-recorded sounds/samples may not be used during the performance.
  • 8. During the prelims, the bands will be given a time period of fifteen minutes, inclusive of sound check.
  • 9. The bands selected for the finals will be provided with a time period of twenty-five minutes, inclusive of sound check. The same original composition can be played in both the prelims and finals.
  • 10. Drummers are requested to bring their own pedals, sticks, extra cymbals and stands if required.
  • 11. Use of alcohol or intoxicative substances is strictly prohibited inside the campus. Any member of a band found in possession with such substances will be immediately disqualified, along with the band.
  • 12. Decision of judges will be final and irrevocable.

Hammer On

Registrations Open!

Hammer-On is the online solo guitar competition where guitarists from all over the world submit their online entries to the backing tracks provided by the crew. Every year, a renowned artist judges the event. The blues maestro Rudy Wallang will be judging the competition and selecting the winner this year.

Hammer On

Rules and Regulations

  1. 1. The tracks for hammer on are Track1 and Track2.
  2. 2. The contestants may select either one of the tracks provided.
  3. 3. Each contestant can submit only one entry.
  4. 4. The length of the entry should not exceed the length of the backing track.
  5. 5. All compositions must be original.
  6. 6. The top 10 entries as selected by the judge are eligible for the people's choice awards.
  7. 7. The judge's choice award will be decided by the judge and his decision shall be final.
  8. 8. The contestants are required to upload their video in a cloud storage platform like Google Drive
    and share the same link in the form ahead. The videos will be uploaded only on the Thundermarch
    YouTube Channel and should not be uploaded on the contestants personal channel.
